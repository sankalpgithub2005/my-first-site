BASIC PARAGRAPH WRITING

A paragraph is a distinct and separate piece of writing that forms part of an overall text.

Each paragraph contains one key idea or topic and at least one controlling idea.

What are 4 types of paragraphs?

The 4 types of paragraphs are:

1. A narrative paragraph which tells a story of a certain event.

2. A descriptive paragraph which gives details about a person, place thing or idea.

3. An expository paragraph which explains something, gives directions or shows how something happens .

4. A persuasive paragraph which is opinionated and tries to sway the reader.
